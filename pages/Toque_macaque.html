Toque macaque

The toque macaque ( Macaca sinica ) is a reddish-brown-coloured Old World monkey endemic to Sri Lanka , where it is known as the rilewa or rilawa (Sinhala රිළවා), (hence "rillow" in the Oxford English Dictionary ).
