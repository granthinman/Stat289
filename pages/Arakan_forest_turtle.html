Arakan forest turtle

The Arakan forest turtle ( Heosemys depressa ) is a rare turtle species which lives only in the Arakan Hills of western Myanmar [1] and the bordering Chittagong Hill Tracts of Bangladesh . [3] [4] [5] The Arakan forest turtle is a semiterrestrial turtle, meaning it can survive in aquatic as well as terrestrial habitats, but as adults, they prefer living in terrestrial habitats. [6]
