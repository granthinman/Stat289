The Black Rhinoceros ( Diceros bicornis ) is a mammal of the Perissodactyla order which lives in the eastern areas of Africa including Kenya , Tanzania , Cameroon , South Africa , Namibia and Zimbabwe . The black rhinoceros is on the endangered species list primarily due to excessive hunting for their horns, which are used mainly in Asian medications although their health benefits have not been proven scientifically.
