Blue whale

The blue whale ( Balaenoptera musculus ) is a marine mammal belonging to the baleen whale parvorder, Mysticeti . [5] At up to 29.9 metres (98 ft) [6] in length and with a maximum recorded weight of 173 tonnes (190 short tons ), [6] it is the largest animal known to have ever existed. [7] [8]
