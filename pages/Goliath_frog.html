Goliath frog

The goliath frog otherwise known as goliath bullfrog or giant slippery frog ( Conraua goliath ) is the largest living frog on Earth. [2] Specimens can grow up to 32 cm (12.6 in) in length from snout to vent , and weigh up to 3.25 kg (7.17 lb). This species has a relatively small habitat range in Cameroon and Equatorial Guinea . Its numbers are dwindling due to habitat destruction and its collection for food and the pet trade.
